Foz do Arelho is a portuguese village in Caldas da Rainha.
It is known as a holiday destination, especially for having a particularly peculiar beach: part of it is bathed by the Atlantic Ocean, while another by the Óbidos Lagoon.

In addition to providing pleasant sea (and sun) bathing, the beach allows you to enjoy a magnificent landscape.

The wide sandy beach of Foz do Arelho beach is bordered by a promenade that allows you to walk, have a drink, drink a coffee or have an ice cream, both day and night, due to the esplanade cafes.
The Obidos Lagoon offers water sports such as kitesurfing, windsurfing or paddle boarding, and its dense vegetation and vast wildlife make strolls very enjoyable.

But not only for bathing tourism and water sports is Foz do Arelho. In addition there are many monuments and points of interest to visit.
