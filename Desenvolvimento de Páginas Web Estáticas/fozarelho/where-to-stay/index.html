In Foz do Arelho, there is no shortage of great places to stay and enjoy your holidays in comfort and without worries.
Here are some suggestions.
Água d'Alma Hotel
Situated between Óbidos Lagoon and the Atlantic Ocean, this contemporary-style hotel occupies a privileged position in Foz do Arelho.
Rooms include cable TV, a desk, and a private bathroom with a bath and shower.
Guests can relax with a drink at the bar, which has a large aquarium with tropical fish.
There is also a modern lounge with a TV to watch news or sporting events.
Hillside House - Suites & Spa
Hillside House Suites & Spa is characterized by its relaxed atmosphere, sophisticated design and the Óbidos Lagoon as a backdrop.
Orbitur Foz do Arelho Campsite
Orbitur Foz do Arelho Campsite has a restaurant and free WiFi.
The campsite has a seasonal outdoor pool.
It is recommended for an economical vacation, in full communion with nature, relaxed, and full of adventure.
